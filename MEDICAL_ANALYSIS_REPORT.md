### Understanding Cervical Cord Compression

**What is it?**
Pressure on the spinal cord in the neck region, can cause:
- Weakness/numbness in arms and legs
- Balance problems
- Difficulty with fine motor tasks
- Bowel/bladder dysfunction (severe cases)

**Why surgery?**
When conservative treatment fails and symptoms progress, surgical decompression prevents permanent spinal cord damage.

**Prognosis after ACDF:**
